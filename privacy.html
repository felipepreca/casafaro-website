Privacy Policy

Effective April 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to casafaro.com (the "Site"), operated by Faro Casa S.A. de C.V. (Amatitán, Jalisco, México) together with its United States affiliate Aura Spirits Group LLC (Texas, United States) (together, "Casa Faro," "we," "our," or "us"). Casa Faro respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you visit the Site. By accessing the Site, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information we collect

Information you provide

When you contact us by email at luis@casafaro.com, we receive the information you include in your message, including your name, email address, and the contents of your inquiry.

Information collected automatically

When you visit the Site, our hosting provider (Vercel Inc.) automatically logs standard technical data, including your IP address, browser type, device type, referring URL, approximate geographic region, and the pages you view. This information is used solely to operate and secure the Site.

Age verification

When you first visit the Site, you are asked to confirm that you are of legal drinking age in your jurisdiction. Your confirmation is stored locally in your browser (using localStorage) and is not transmitted to us. No personal information is collected during age verification.

Cookies and similar technologies

The Site does not currently use advertising cookies, analytics cookies, or third-party tracking technologies. We use only essential local storage to remember your age verification.

2. How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and communicate with you.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the Site.
  • Protect against fraud, abuse, and security incidents.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. How we share information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers. With hosting, email, and infrastructure providers (currently Vercel Inc., GoDaddy LLC, and Cloudflare, Inc.) who process information on our behalf under confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal obligations. When required by applicable law, subpoena, or other legal process, or to protect our rights and the safety of others.
  • Business transfers. In connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, subject to standard confidentiality protections.

4. Data retention

We retain email correspondence for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and maintain our business records, typically no longer than seven (7) years. Server logs are typically retained by our hosting provider for no more than 30 days. You may request deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting luis@casafaro.com.

5. Your rights

All visitors

You may contact us at luis@casafaro.com to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe consistent with applicable law.

California residents (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to: (i) know what personal information we collect; (ii) request deletion of your personal information; (iii) correct inaccurate personal information; and (iv) opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information. We do not sell your personal information. To exercise these rights, email luis@casafaro.com with the subject line "California Privacy Request."

European Economic Area & United Kingdom (GDPR / UK GDPR)

If you are located in the EEA or the UK, our lawful bases for processing are: (i) your consent (where applicable); (ii) the performance of a contract or pre-contractual steps; and (iii) our legitimate interests in operating the Site. You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, object to, or port your personal data, and the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. Email luis@casafaro.com to exercise these rights.

Mexico residents (LFPDPPP)

If you are located in Mexico, you have the rights of Acceso, Rectificacion, Cancelacion, and Oposicion (ARCO) with respect to your personal data, as well as the right to revoke consent. To exercise these rights, email luis@casafaro.com.

6. International data transfers

Our infrastructure providers may process your data in the United States. Where required by law, appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses apply to transfers of personal data from the EEA, UK, or Mexico to the United States.

7. Children

The Site is not directed to anyone under the legal drinking age in their jurisdiction, and under no circumstances directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you believe we have collected information from a minor, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including TLS encryption for all Site traffic, access controls on our email systems, and secure infrastructure providers. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Effective" date above. Material changes will be noted on the Site.
